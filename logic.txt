TimeLog – Product Requirements Document (PRD)
1. Overview

TimeLog is a simple daily time logging application.

The goal is not to automatically track the user's activity. Instead, users manually record what they are doing throughout the day using a clean table interface.

At the end of each day, the system automatically closes the day (e.g., at 11:59 PM or a configurable time) and generates a productivity report and daily insights.

2. Goal

Help users answer one simple question:

"Where did my time go today?"

No complicated timers.

No Pomodoro.

No AI scheduling.

Just simple logging and meaningful daily insights.

7. Automatic Day Closing

The user never presses Finish Day.

At the configured end-of-day time (default 11:59 PM):

Today's entries are locked.
Daily report is generated automatically.
A new empty day is created.
If the last activity has no end time, the app prompts the user next time they open it to complete or adjust it.

20. Design Philosophy

The app should feel like Google Sheets meets Apple Health:

Clean table-based input.
No clutter.
No unnecessary timers.
No complex productivity systems.
Focus on logging first, insights second.

The goal is to make daily logging take less than 30 seconds per entry, while providing an automatic end-of-day summary that helps users understand how they actually spent their time and whether it was productive.
